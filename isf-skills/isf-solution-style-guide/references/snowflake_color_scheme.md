# Snowflake Brand Color Scheme (2025)

## Primary Colors

| Role              | Name           | Hex       | Usage                                      |
|-------------------|----------------|-----------|--------------------------------------------|
| Primary           | Snowflake Blue | `#29B5E8` | Primary brand color, buttons, key accents  |
| Secondary         | Mid-Blue       | `#11567F` | Headers, strong accents                    |
| Light Accent      | Star Blue      | `#75CDD7` | Backgrounds, subtle highlights             |

## Extended / Accent Colors

| Role        | Name           | Hex       | Usage                                |
|------------|----------------|-----------|--------------------------------------|
| Accent     | Valencia Orange| `#FF9F36` | Warnings, attention, CTAs            |
| Accent     | Purple Moon    | `#7254A3` | Premium / special highlights         |
| Accent     | Firstlight     | `#D45B90` | Creative elements, design accents    |

## Neutrals

| Role        | Name        | Hex       | Usage                             |
|------------|-------------|-----------|-----------------------------------|
| Dark Text  | Midnight    | `#000000` | Titles, dark text                 |
| Body Text  | Medium Gray | `#5B5B5B` | Body and secondary text           |
| Background | White       | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, text on dark colors  |
